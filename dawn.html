Rolls-Royce Dawn

The Ultimate Open-Air Luxury Experience

About the Dawn

The Rolls-Royce Dawn is the world's most luxurious open-air motor car—an ultra-luxury convertible grand tourer built to offer an effortless open-air driving experience without compromise. It redefines the meaning of convertible motoring for the ultra-high net-worth individual.

Despite being a convertible, the Dawn is heavily engineered to maintain the whisper-quiet cabin that Rolls-Royce is renowned for when the fabric roof is up. The advanced power soft-top retracts in just 22 seconds and operates seamlessly whether the car is stationary or in motion.

The striking gold wrap available on select models emphasizes the Dawn's reputation as a glamorous, attention-grabbing statement car that turns heads wherever it travels. Every detail, from the hand-stitched leather to the bespoke Starlight Headliner, reinforces the uncompromising commitment to luxury.
