About Rolls-Royce

Rolls-Royce Motor Cars is the ultimate global symbol of automotive luxury and handcrafted British excellence, operating today out of its iconic headquarters in Goodwood, England, as a subsidiary of the BMW Group. Founded in 1904 by Charles Rolls and Henry Royce, the marque is defined by legendary innovations like its ultra-quiet "Magic Carpet Ride" suspension, the iconic retracting Spirit of Ecstasy hood ornament, and custom Bespoke options like the fiber-optic Starlight Headliner. From the commanding presence of its flagship Phantom sedan and the rugged luxury of the Cullinan SUV, to its groundbreaking new all-electric Spectre super-coupé, every vehicle is custom-tailored to offer unmatched presence, near-silent performance, and the absolute pinnacle of elite status on the road.
