The Ultimate Symbol of Automotive Luxury

About the Phantom

As the flagship model of the Rolls-Royce brand, the Phantom represents the absolute pinnacle of automotive luxury, scale, and status. Since its introduction in 1925, the Phantom has been synonymous with uncompromising elegance, exceptional craftsmanship, and cutting-edge engineering.

The Phantom is famous for its legendary "Magic Carpet Ride"—an advanced air suspension system that actively scans the road 2,700 times per second to isolate passengers from any bumps or imperfections. This results in a whisper-quiet, supremely smooth driving experience that redefines what luxury means.

Every element of the Phantom is hand-crafted by master artisans. The iconic retracting Spirit of Ecstasy hood ornament, the leather-stitched dashboard, and the fiber-optic Starlight Headliner (over 1,000 fiber-optic lights) showcase the attention to detail that defines Rolls-Royce excellence.
